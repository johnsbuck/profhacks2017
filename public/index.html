FAQ



Who can attend?

Any high school, undergraduate and graduate students are welcome to attend.

How will you decide between applicants?

Applications will be reviewed by committee and selected participants will and be notified via email. Rowan students are guaranteed acceptance to this event, but will still need to apply.

How much is this going to cost me?

ProfHacks is completely free! Food, drinks, and awesome swag are completely free of charge!

How do I get there?

From Philly

You can drive to Glassboro by taking NJ 42 to NJ 55 to US 322 at exit 50B.

You can take NJTransit bus.

Outside Philly

Consider using US Megabus.

You may also want to use Amtrak.

Where should I park?

You can park in Parking Lot D, right next to the engineering building.

What about team sizes?

The maximum number of people a team can have is four. Though encouraged, having a team beforehand is not necessary. If you want to form a team at ProfHacks, there will be a fun mixer beforehand to help you meet potential team members.

What should I bring?

You should bring some form of identification (driverâ€™s license, school ID card, etc.), laptops, phones, chargers and sleeping gear. You will also need to sign a liability waiver and photo release form to gain admittance.

What shouldnâ€™t I bring?

Tools such as power drills, Dremel drills and the like are prohibited. Articles such as weapons and alcohol are also prohibited. Any competitor found to be in possession of these articles will be disqualified from the competition.

Do we have travel reimbursement?

We will be offering travel reimbursement of up to $50 per person. You will be able to write your reason in the registration form and we will be picking based on whoever registers first. If you have a question, we recommend registering first and sending us an e-mail via our contact form.
